ABOUT US

Delivering Incredible Taste since 2025!
Renowned as “Malivada Road” wala BKB stores, we are Creame & Crust Cakes and Pastry, the very first commercial bakery shop of Junagadh. With a portfolio of 150+ products baked to perfection with years of experience and expertise, we believe in delivering quality products that are fine-tuned to the taste and likings of our Gujaratis.

With our commitment to hygiene and change, we have made space in hearts of all the generations and with the flair for innovation, we have created products that resonate with people of all age groups.

We at Creame & Crust inherit the soul of our city and just like Junagadh, we have the best of both worlds for our customers. Because from “Khari to Creame & Crust Ni J” to “Biscuits and Cookies of BK are so awesome!” our ability to adapt to the expectations of customers has helped us stand the testament of time.
